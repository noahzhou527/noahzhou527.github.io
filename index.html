Why we chose the Go Climbers track

Climbing gives us a strong design space where progress, challenge, and social connection all matter at the same time.

We chose the "Go Climbers" track because climbing uniquely blends fitness, self-improvement, and community. Its clear progression system—through routes and grades—makes it ideal for human-centred design focused on sustained user engagement.

At the same time, current climbing experiences are often fragmented. Progress may be remembered informally, training is frequently tracked across disconnected tools, and social participation depends heavily on being physically present at the gym or already knowing other climbers. This creates barriers for beginners who want structure, for regular climbers who want clearer progression, and for socially motivated users who want to find partners, events, and encouragement more easily.

From a design perspective, climbing inherently involves challenge, exploration, and mastery, making it a perfect fit for a playful digital system. Our mobile app aims to translate these elements into digital interactions to boost motivation, visualize progress, and enhance social connectedness.

Value Proposition

Designed for beginners, useful for committed climbers

rockbond lowers the barrier for first-time climbers while still giving intermediate users a better way to plan sessions, track goals, and connect with compatible partners.

Primary Need

Confidence

New climbers often do not know where to start, how to read a route, or when to ask for help.

Secondary Need

Momentum

Regular climbers want progress that feels meaningful, social, and motivating instead of repetitive.
