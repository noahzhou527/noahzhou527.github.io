Novel Concept

Progress, memory, and social motivation in one loop

The concept focuses on making each climbing session feel connected to the next one, while keeping the experience inclusive, playful, and low-pressure.

This project proposes a hybrid, human-centred climbing ecosystem that combines personal progress tracking with low-pressure social motivation through playful interactions. Rather than behaving like a generic fitness tracker, rockbond turns climbing into a continuous journey that carries meaning across sessions.

At its core are Guided Progress Quests, where each session contributes to a visible progression loop. This is extended through Climb Circles for small-group encouragement without competitive pressure, and Exploration-Based Discovery that rewards re-engagement with the wider climbing community through lightweight incentives.

Supporting features such as Quick Session Memory and Beginner Confidence Prompts help users remember where they left off, reduce uncertainty, and keep the experience approachable. The novelty is not any single feature alone, but how progress, memory, and social interaction work together as mutually reinforcing layers within one experience.

Value Proposition

Designed for beginners, useful for committed climbers

rockbond lowers the barrier for first-time climbers with confidence prompts and quick memory cues, while still giving regular climbers a richer way to track goals, sustain momentum, and connect with compatible partners.

Primary Need

Confidence

New climbers often do not know where to start, how to read a route, or when to ask for help.

Secondary Need

Momentum

Regular climbers want progress that feels meaningful, social, and motivating instead of repetitive.
