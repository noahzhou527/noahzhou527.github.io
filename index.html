Project Snapshot

A more social, guided, and playful way to climb

Instead of behaving like a generic fitness tracker, CRAG focuses on the real climbing experience: route confusion, confidence gaps, training motivation, and finding the right partners.

Value Proposition

Designed for beginners, useful for committed climbers

CRAG lowers the barrier for first-time climbers while still giving intermediate users a better way to plan sessions, track goals, and connect with compatible partners.

Primary Need

Confidence

New climbers often do not know where to start, how to read a route, or when to ask for help.

Secondary Need

Momentum

Regular climbers want progress that feels meaningful, social, and motivating instead of repetitive.
