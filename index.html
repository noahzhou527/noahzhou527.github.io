User Personas

Understanding who we're building for — their goals, frustrations, and real-world scenarios.

Alex Chen

Novice Boulderer

🧗‍♂️ Demographics

  • Age & Occupation: 25, Software Engineer
  • Experience: 3 months (Climbs V2-V3 indoors)
  • Location: Urban city, climbs at local indoor gyms

🎯 Goals

  • Wants to build upper body strength and overcome fear of heights.
  • Looking to make new friends in the climbing community.
  • Needs to learn proper footwork technique instead of just using arm strength.

😟 Frustrations

  • Intimidated by complex climbing jargon and experienced climbers.
  • Struggles with tracking personal progress and remembering routes.
  • Finger skin hurts easily, doesn't know how to properly rest/recover.

📍 Scenario

  • Goes to the gym twice a week after work. Usually stares at a boulder problem not knowing how to start, wishing there was an easy guide or community app to show the "beta" (solution) for beginners.

Sarah Jenkins

Lead Climbing Expert

🧗‍♀️ Demographics

  • Age & Occupation: 31, Freelance Designer / Outdoor Guide
  • Experience: 8 years (Projects 5.13a / V8 outdoors)
  • Location: Lives near mountains, travels for outdoor crags

🎯 Goals

  • Projecting hard outdoor routes and pushing physical limits.
  • Wants detailed topographical maps (Topos) for obscure, hidden climbing areas.
  • Needs to find reliable, safety-conscious belay partners for weekend trips.

😟 Frustrations

  • Unexpected weather changes ruining a planned outdoor climbing weekend.
  • Lack of updated, accurate route conditions (e.g., loose rocks, wet holds) online.
  • Standard fitness apps don't track specialized climbing metrics like hangboard time.

📍 Scenario

  • Planning a 3-day trip to a limestone cave. Needs a platform to check micro-climate weather forecasts, download offline route maps, and read recent comments from other advanced climbers about the rock condition.
