Playful Features

Three features for a more guided, playful, and social climb

These are the three must-have features for the concept: they are simple enough to prototype in a web app while still clearly expressing the project's playful direction.

Feature 01

Route Quest

Turns a gym session into a challenge-driven experience instead of a vague workout.

  • Choose routes by level, style, or goal
  • Log completion and failed attempts
  • Unlock badges, streaks, and progress markers
Feature 02

Beta Buddy

Offers hint-based route guidance that feels approachable for beginners.

  • Step-by-step hints instead of walls of text
  • Visual prompts for start holds and body position
  • Community tips that do not feel overly expert-heavy
Feature 03

Climb Circle

Adds lightweight social coordination so users can find partners and shared momentum.

  • Post sessions by time, gym, and level
  • Join a climb or mark interest
  • Build accountability and a sense of belonging
Why these features

Each feature responds to a different human need

Together, the three features cover confidence, progress, and connection - the areas most likely to affect whether users enjoy climbing and keep coming back.

Confidence

Beta Buddy

Reduces intimidation by helping users understand what to do without forcing them into expert language or public asking.

Motivation

Route Quest

Makes progress feel visible through goals, levels, and playful feedback that users can see after each session.

Belonging

Climb Circle

Helps users feel less alone, which is especially important for beginners trying to turn climbing into a habit.
